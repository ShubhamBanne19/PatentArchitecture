India's Innovation Paradox

India invents. India doesn't patent. This book is here to change that.

India is the world's third-largest startup ecosystem. Yet Indian companies file patents at a fraction of the rate of Chinese, Korean, or German peers. This is not a talent problem. It is an IP culture problem - and it has a structural cause.

The Patent Architect is written with a mission: to put rigorous IP knowledge in the hands of the practitioners, founders, and educators who can change this ratio. The book is priced for access, not profit. The website is free. The mission is real.
